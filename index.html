FReD Annotator

Annotate reference lists with replication info

Check which studies in a reference list, journal article or reading list have been replicated.

  • Paste DOIs or upload files (PDF, BibTeX, RIS)
  • Automatically match to FReD database
  • View replication outcomes at a glance
  • Generate shareable reports
  • Check for retracted studies

About FReD

🔍

Comprehensive

Covers replications from major projects like Many Labs, Psychological Science Accelerator, and individual studies.

📊

Multiple Criteria

Assess replication success using 8 different statistical criteria, from simple significance to Small Telescopes.

🌐

Open Science

Part of FORRT (Framework for Open and Reproducible Research Training). All data openly available.

📝

Easy Annotation

Quickly annotate syllabi and reading lists to show students which findings have been replicated.
